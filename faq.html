Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Sports Mahotsav 2026.

Go to the Games page, click "Register Now" on your desired sport, and fill out the Google Form with your details. Make sure to submit the form before the registration deadline.

The registration deadline is March 7, 2026. Late registrations will not be accepted under any circumstances. We encourage students to register as soon as possible to secure their spot.

All currently enrolled students of Katpur College Patan across all semesters and branches are eligible to participate. A valid college ID card is mandatory to take part in any event.

Yes, students from all semesters (1st to 6th) and all branches are welcome to participate. There is no semester or branch restriction for any of the sports events.

Sports Mahotsav 2026 includes Cricket, Kabaddi, and Tug Of War. More events may be added — stay updated via the college notice board and this website.

All events will be held within the Katpur College Patan campus. Cricket will be at the main cricket ground, Kabaddi at the indoor court, and Tug Of War at the main ground. Detailed venue maps will be shared before the event.

The events are scheduled as follows: Tug Of War – March 10, Kabaddi – March 11, Cricket – March 12, 2026. All events start at 9:00 AM. Please report 30 minutes early.

You must carry your college ID card at all times. Sports shoes and proper sports attire (college uniform preferred) are mandatory. Equipment such as balls and ropes will be provided by the organizers.

Disqualification may occur for rule violations, misconduct, or not reporting on time. The referee's decision is final. Disqualified teams will not be eligible for prizes or certificates for that event.
