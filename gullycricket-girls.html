About the Event

Gully Cricket (Girls) is an exciting informal cricket format tailor-made for college campuses. Teams of 6–8 play short, high-energy matches with simplified rules. It's the perfect opportunity for girls to enjoy the spirit of cricket in a fun, competitive and inclusive setting!

Rules & Guidelines

  • check_circle6–8 players per team; minimum 6 to field.
  • check_circle5 overs per innings; no-balls and wides counted.
  • check_circleTennis ball used; no protective gear required.
  • check_circleAll players must bowl at least 1 over.
  • check_circleCollege ID required on event day.
